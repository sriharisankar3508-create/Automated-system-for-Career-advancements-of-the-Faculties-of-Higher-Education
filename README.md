# 🎓 Automated System for Career Advancements of the Faculties of Higher Education

## 📖 Overview
This web-based system helps higher education institutions 
to automatically track and manage faculty career 
advancements including publications, achievements, 
promotions, and professional development activities.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- ✅ To digitize faculty career records
- ✅ To automate promotion eligibility tracking
- ✅ To provide admin dashboard for monitoring
- ✅ To generate reports for management decisions

---

## ✨ Features
- 🔐 Faculty Login & Profile Management
- 📄 Research Publication Tracking
- 🏆 Awards & Achievement Records
- 📈 Promotion Eligibility Checker
- 📊 Admin Dashboard & Analytics
- 📥 Report Generation

---
